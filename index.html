Instructions
  1. One of Batter or Umpire flash at center place and a Outfielder will flash at random place.
  2. You have to keep in mind that a batter is shown or a umpire and the position of Outfielder on screen.
  3. You will have to choose right player and right position same as you saw in flash.
  4. If both are correct you will gain a point otherwise game over.
  5. As you are scoring more, speed of the flash will increase with each score.
